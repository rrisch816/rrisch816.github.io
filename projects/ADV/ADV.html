Project 1: Improving Upon a Found Plot

I found this plot on instagram (from European Magazine) that I thought needed to be redone. I felt that the colors in Europe Magazine's plot were an added level of unnecessary dimension - the colors didn't mean anything, they were just arbitrarily picked in 10s. Instead, in accordance with minimalism and intentional design choicesI made my plot colored with a gradient representative to the actual cost of living index of each country. I didn't think the radial aspect was necessary either, so my bar chart is stacked straight. I also included a plot to compare population density vs cost of living index by European region.
